Raw price = global avg unit value (USD/t) = world coffee export revenue ÷ volume (FAO 5922 & 5910, items 656+657). Processed = raw × 1.35 (fixed 35% roasting premium — no separate FAO price series for roasted). Import prices (dashed) = export prices + 8% freight & insurance estimate. Shaded area = value-added gap. Cursor = year slider.
Coffee value chain — Producers · Processors · Consumers
Producers → Processors: raw green bean exports (FAO item 656). Processors → Consumers: roasted/processed coffee exports only (FAO item 657). Processors = countries where ≥30% of coffee exports are roasted — a threshold chosen at the natural break in the data (UK 37% vs Belgium 19%). Flow width = share of each column's world total.
